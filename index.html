Short Bio:

Bodong Chen is an Assistant Professor in the College of Education and Human Development at the University of Minnesota. His research is at the intersection of CSCL, learning analytics, networked learning, and network analysis. Bodong’s work is broadly published in premier venues and funded by the NSF CRII, the Digital Learning for Development initiative, etc. Bodong holds a Bachelor of Science from Beijing Normal University, a Master of Education from Peking University, and a Ph.D. from OISE/University of Toronto.

See CoLIG for Bodong’s research projects.

陈伯栋，四川人，明尼苏达大学、教育与人类发展学院助理教授。

主要研究方向包括：计算机支持的协作学习、学习分析学、网络分析、网络学习。

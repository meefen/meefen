  I am an associate professor in [Learning Sciences and Technologies](https://www.gse.upenn.edu/academics/programs/learning-sciences-technology-phd) at the [University of Pennsylvania Graduate School of Education](https://www.gse.upenn.edu/).  My research is at the intersection of the learning sciences, AI, learning analytics, and network science. As a learning scientist and educational technologist, I strive to make learning a meaningful part of social participation for people of all backgrounds and circumstances. My scholarly inquiry integrates knowledge media design, software engineering, and data science methods to continually improve infrastructures for learning. Guided by design-based research and participatory design approaches, I aim to generate justice-oriented pedagogical designs, technological innovations, and empirical understandings of learning in authentic settings. 
  
  I am currently an Associate Editor of *[ijCSCL](https://link.springer.com/journal/11412)* and sit on the editorial boards of a number of journals, including [*Journal of the Learning Sciences*](https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/hlns20) and [*Journal of Learning Analytics*](https://learning-analytics.info/index.php/JLA/index). I was elected to the Executive Committee of the Society for Learning Analytics Research (SoLAR) and the Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning (CSCL) Committee of the International Society of the Learning Sciences (ISLS), and co-chaired of the CSCL Conference in 2022 and 2025. 

  Before joining Penn GSE, I was an associate professor in the College of Education and Human Development and founding co-director of the Learning Informatics Lab at the University of Minnesota.
